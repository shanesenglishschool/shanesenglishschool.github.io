Frequently Asked Questions

How does the AI technology enhance learning?

Our AI technology creates personalized exercises tailored to your specific learning needs, tracks your progress in real-time, and adapts the difficulty and focus areas based on your performance. This ensures you're always challenged at the right level and working on areas that need the most improvement.

What makes your teaching method special for Spanish speakers?

After 25 years of teaching Spanish speakers, I understand exactly where you struggle - pronunciation patterns, grammar structures that don't exist in Spanish, and cultural communication differences. My methods specifically address these challenges with targeted exercises and explanations.
