Frequently Asked Questions

How does the AI technology enhance learning?

Our AI technology creates personalized exercises tailored to your specific learning needs, tracks your progress in real-time, and adapts the difficulty and focus areas based on your performance. This ensures you're always challenged at the right level and working on areas that need the most improvement.

What makes your teaching method special for Spanish speakers?

After 25 years of teaching Spanish speakers, I understand exactly where you struggle - pronunciation patterns, grammar structures that don't exist in Spanish, and cultural communication differences. My methods specifically address these challenges with targeted exercises and explanations.

How do the online classes work?

Our online classes are held on a private, secure platform. The curriculum is based on the official Cambridge Assessment Exams for levels B1, B2, and C1. Each class focuses on developing all four key skills required for the exams: Reading, Listening, Writing, and Speaking, ensuring a comprehensive and effective learning experience.

What technology do I need for the classes?

All you need is a stable internet connection, a computer or tablet with a microphone and webcam, and a quiet place to focus. We use a modern, browser-based platform, so no special software installation is required.

How do I pay for the classes?

We offer several convenient payment options to suit your preference. Payments can be made securely via Bizum, direct bank transfer, or PayPal. Further details will be provided upon registration.
